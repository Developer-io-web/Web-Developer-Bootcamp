    Product Description
    X-Class Design

    Featuring a clean and contemporary design with R3 corners, the 3D curved ultra O screen, and the small punch-hole front camera, this phone is a sight to behold.

    Pro-quad Camera

    Everything image you capture with this phone will be gorgeous, thanks to its quad-camera system (48 MP main Gimbal camera + 13 MP professional portrait lens + 8 MP super-wide-angle and macro camera + 8 MP periscope telephoto lens with 60x Hyper Zoom.

    Gimbal Camera System

    This smartphone’s Gimbal camera system will ensure outstanding stabilization so that every picture comes out really well.

    Extreme Night Vision

    Enjoy brighter, clearer, and stable images with this phone, even those that are clicked during the night time.

    Astro Mode

    With the gimbal camera system and an advanced algorithm, this camera removes the smears so you can capture the starry sky at its best.

    Sky Night Filters

    The dreamy city night photos will seem much more beautiful, thanks to this phone’s sky night filters for the urban nightscape.

    Pro Sports Mode

    This camera will help you capture motion shots with little delay, as the combination of Gimbal stabilization and customized sensor will pave the way for quick focus and shorter exposure time.

    Motion AF Tracking

    Choose a subject in any frame and keep it as the focus, with the help of the Motion AF tracking. With the Audio Zoom feature, you can even zoom in on a subject of your choice while taking a video and the sound can be amplified by up to 15 dB.

    60x Hyper Zoom

    The Gimbal camera system and the dedicated periscope lens will help you zoom into your surroundings closely. You can opt for the 5x optical zoom and the 60x hyper zoom.

    50 mm Professional Lens

    With the help of this smartphone’s professional lens, you can get set for natural and authentic portraits clicked during the day and night.

    Super Wide-angle

    The 8 MP super-wide-angle camera lets you capture larger landscapes and even have more people fit into the frame.

    Smooth Display

    Every visual will appear vividly on this smartphone’s 3D Curved Ultra O screen. It has a 90 Hz refresh rate and 180 Hz response rate.

    Powerful Performance

    Featuring the Qualcomm Snapdragon 765G mobile platform and 8 GB of RAM, this smartphone will ensure that you get to enjoy a seamless performance. Oh, and it features a memory capacity of up to 256 GB to help you store your pictures, files, and other things. It also supports 5G connectivity.

    Vivo FlashCharge 2.0

    With the help of the Vivo FlashCharge 2.0, you can ensure that your phone is charged in a jiffy.

    Hi-Res Certification

    This smartphone is powered by the AK4377A independent audio chip which lets you immerse yourself into the world of your favourite tunes.
